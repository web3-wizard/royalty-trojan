Privacy Policy for Royalty Trojan

Last updated: April 11, 2026

Overview

Royalty Trojan enables creator support flows on YouTube, X, and Twitch by resolving creator wallet information and opening Solana payment interactions through the user's wallet provider.

Data We Process

  • Visited creator page URLs on supported platforms to detect creator identity.
  • Public creator identifiers (for example handle/channel ID) and optional website domain links shown publicly on creator pages.
  • User-selected configuration stored locally in extension storage, including IDENTITY_SERVICE_URL and cache settings.
  • Locally cached wallet resolution results in extension storage to improve performance.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect passwords, seed phrases, or private keys.
  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We do not maintain user accounts in this extension.

Third-Party Services

The extension may call the configured Identity Service endpoint and public blockchain-related infrastructure (for example DNS-over-HTTPS, Nostr relays, and Solana RPC providers) to resolve creator wallet information and support payment workflows.

Storage

Data is stored locally in browser extension storage (chrome.storage) and may include cached wallet lookups with a time-to-live expiration.

Security

Transactions require explicit user confirmation in the wallet provider. The extension does not directly custody user funds.

Your Choices

  • You can remove stored extension data by clearing extension storage or uninstalling the extension.
  • You can change the identity service URL from extension options.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the publisher via the store listing support link.
